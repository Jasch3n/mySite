July 12, 2020

A Storm is Coming

A cloud curiously wandered alone
When it saw a man dressed in red
Lying on a sunlit meadow as bed.
Among the bright yellow flowers,
He claimed his throne.

The loners stared at each other
And did nothing but breathe;
The man tried to say something, beneath,
But gave up and turned towards the flowers
That reminded him of his brother.

A golden crown lay beside his head,
Which was now perfectly still
The cloud looked over the hill
And saw more men dressed in red
Lying on the sunlit meadow as bed.

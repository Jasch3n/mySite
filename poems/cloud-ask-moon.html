The Cloud Asked the Moon

Moon!
How looks the world from the summit of heaven?
Do you see all the looks of admiration
Piercing through the clouds?
Do you cherish all that is at rest in relaxation,
Under your light, dreaming aloud?
Do you smell the hipocrisy in conversations,
Where lovers take their hollow vows?
Do you extend your embrace in exploration,
Lighting the way to those who frown?

What? I only see a blue sphere!
Answered the Moon.

July 19, 2020

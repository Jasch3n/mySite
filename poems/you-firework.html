You, or Firework

You are a bright blossom
when all around is wither and darkness.
You are a radiant rainbow
after a shelterless night in the storm.
You see gold and glitter
in the worthless and dull.
You hear tones and harmony
in the noisy and dissonant.
But you rose to my sky like firework,
turning into a transient flourish of light,
beautiful and elegant,
